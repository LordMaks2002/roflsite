Quantum Physics

The science of the smallest particles

Quantum physics is a branch of science that studies the behavior of matter and energy at the atomic and subatomic levels. It explores phenomena such as superposition, entanglement, and wave-particle duality, which challenge our classical understanding of reality.

This is black hole

A black hole is an astronomical object with a gravitational pull so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape. They are formed when massive stars collapse and have a boundary called the event horizon, beyond which escape is impossible. The term can also be used metaphorically to describe things that are great consumers or voids, such as a financial black hole.
